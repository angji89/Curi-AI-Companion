Companionship without purpose.

Tablets and voice assistants with structured content — but at a steep cost. Prolonged screen exposure harms developing eyes, reduces attention span, and disrupts sleep.

Screen Damage Passive One-Size-Fits-All

The burden falls entirely on the parent.

No Insights Fragmented

Pedagogical Foundation

The UK's national
standard for early learning.

CURI is built on the Early Years Foundation Stage — the statutory framework set by the UK's Department for Education that governs learning, development, and care for every child from birth to age 5 in England. It is followed by every Ofsted-registered nursery, pre-school, childminder, and reception class in the country. Not our opinion of what children should learn. The national standard.
